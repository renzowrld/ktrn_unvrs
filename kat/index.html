Katrina Gale

a Filipino-Canadian who uses multi-media to create animations

Katrina Gale H. is an Experimental Animation student, born in Bulacan, Philippines and raised in Toronto, ON. Gale uses mixed media and explores the boundaries of traditional and digital medium in her animation work including stop-motion, 2d and 3d animation. In her works, she subtly tackles societal and philosophical issues in her visual narrative storytelling. Gale aims to produce a mixed-media short film for Ottawa International Animation Film Festival.
